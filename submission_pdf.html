💡 Idea Summary
A platform where small/local clothing store owners list their inventory online, and nearby shoppers can discover stores, browse styles, check real-time stock availability, and place orders from multiple shops — all from one place instead of visiting stores physically.
⚠️ The Challenge Being Solved
  • Customers waste significant time visiting multiple stores physically to compare styles, sizes, and prices
  • Store inventories are not visible online — no way to check what's available before visiting
  • Size and stock information is often unavailable until the customer reaches the store
  • Local/small clothing shops steadily lose customers to large online marketplaces (Myntra, Amazon, Ajio)
